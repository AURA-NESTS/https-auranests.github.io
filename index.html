VISMUN

VISMUN (Vista International School Model United Nations) is an academic simulation of the United Nations. High school students from top schools in Hyderabad participate as delegates, representing different countries and debating global issues. It's an educational event designed to foster critical thinking, public speaking, diplomacy and global awareness among students. Beyond academic enrichment, VISMUN serves as an invaluable opportunity for students to enhance their leadership abilities, teamwork, and adaptability. It not only prepares them for future roles in international relations, law, public policy and governance but also nurtures a sense of responsibility and awareness toward global challenges

Thank you for reading this and visiting our website, Welcome to VISMUN 2025!

Regards,

Harshitha Andra,
